-Magic Wardrobe-
Acts as a questline, where you can unlock and claim quest rewards for vanity.

-Vanity-
Some vanities have special effects, like sprouting mushrooms when stationary or smoke when moving

Accessories that you can wear to create particles or other visual effects when you move
